# Best Practices

- Running meeting as a work session - ensure you have working items for the meeting, work on the items live in the meeting, send out a meeting summary + recording with what was accomplished.

- Rules to keep your meetings as working sessions - send the agenda and working items before the meeting, block your calendar 10 minutes before the meeting you are running, get into the meeting 2 minutes early to share your screen, set meeting time to 25 to 50 minutes so that it overruns it doesn't cut persons next meeting.
