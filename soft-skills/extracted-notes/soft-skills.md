# Soft Skills

## Meetings

- Running meeting as a work session - ensure you have working items for the meeting, work on the items live in the meeting, send out a meeting summary + recording with what was accomplished.

- Rules to keep your meetings as working sessions - send the agenda and working items before the meeting, block your calendar 10 minutes before the meeting you are running, get into the meeting 2 minutes early to share your screen, set meeting time to 25 to 50 minutes so that it overruns it doesn't cut persons next meeting.

## Carrier

- Skill depth + adaptability is best thing you can do for your secure carrier.

- Managers mentally bucket employees into - (low noise, low ambition), (high noise, emotional), (strategic, growth-oriented).

## Writing

- Types of technical documents by purpose -
  - Explaining documents (explain how something works) like Architecture overviews, design docs.
  - Instructing documents (tell someone how to do something) like setup guides, tutorials, runbooks.
  - Referencing documents (act as reference material) like API reference, configuration docs.
  - Recording decisions (record what was decided and why) like ADRs, change logs, release notes.
